Rolex

Description

Rolex is a popular Ugandan street food made of a chapati rolled with a fried egg and fresh vegetables. It is quick, delicious and filling.

The name "Rolex" comes from "Rolled Eggs" — a favorite snack enjoyed across Uganda.

Ingredients

  • 2 chapatis
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tomato sliced
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1 green pepper sliced
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl and add salt.
  2. Add sliced tomatoes, onions and green pepper to the egg mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour egg mixture into pan and fry until cooked.
  5. Place chapati on top of the egg while still in pan.
  6. Flip and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Remove from pan and roll the chapati with egg inside.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
